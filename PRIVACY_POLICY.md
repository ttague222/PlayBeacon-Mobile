# PlayBeacon Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

PlayBeacon ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information from users of PlayBeacon, a Roblox game discovery app designed for children.

**PlayBeacon is designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and is intended for users of all ages, with special protections for children under 13.**

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use PlayBeacon, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality
- **Usage Data**: Which features you use, games you view, and how you interact with the app (collected in aggregate, not linked to individual users)
- **Error Logs**: Technical information to help us fix bugs and improve the app

### Information You Provide

- **Anonymous Account**: By default, PlayBeacon uses anonymous authentication. No personal information is required to use the app's core features.
- **Wishlist & Collections**: Games you save are stored securely and associated with your anonymous account.
- **Optional Google Sign-In**: If a parent or guardian chooses to enable Google Sign-In, we receive:
  - Email address (for account recovery only)
  - Display name (optional)
  - Profile picture (optional)

**Important: Google Sign-In is parent-gated and requires parental consent for users under 13.**

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and improve the PlayBeacon app experience
- Save your wishlists and game collections
- Personalize game recommendations based on your preferences
- Fix bugs and improve app performance
- Ensure the safety and security of our users

**We do NOT:**
- Sell personal information to third parties
- Use personal information for behavioral advertising to children
- Share personal information with third parties except as described in this policy
- Collect more information than necessary to provide our services

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services
- Anonymous accounts and their data may be deleted after extended periods of inactivity

## Third-Party Services

PlayBeacon uses the following third-party services:

### Google Firebase
- **Purpose**: Authentication, data storage, and app analytics
- **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Google AdMob (COPPA-Compliant)
- **Purpose**: Display non-personalized advertisements
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Note**: We have configured AdMob to serve only child-directed, non-personalized ads

### Roblox (Data Display Only)
- **Purpose**: We display publicly available game information from Roblox
- **Privacy Policy**: https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/115004630823-Roblox-Privacy-Policy
- **Note**: We do not share any user data with Roblox

## Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

1. **Review Information**: Request to review any personal information we have collected from their child
2. **Delete Information**: Request deletion of their child's personal information
3. **Refuse Collection**: Refuse to allow further collection of their child's information
4. **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for Google Sign-In at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

## Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously:

- **No Required Personal Information**: Children can use PlayBeacon without providing any personal information
- **Parent-Gated Features**: Features requiring personal information (like Google Sign-In) require parental involvement
- **No Social Features**: PlayBeacon does not include chat, messaging, or other social features
- **No External Links to Inappropriate Content**: All links are to Roblox game pages only
- **No Behavioral Advertising**: We do not serve personalized ads to children

## Data Retention and Deletion

- **Anonymous Accounts**: Data is retained while the account is active. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 12 months.
- **Google-Linked Accounts**: Data is retained until the user requests deletion.
- **Deletion Requests**: Users can request complete data deletion by contacting us.
